About Us

Pocket Travel is a fast safe tansportation establishment that provides an opportunity for car owners to use their cars even if they are not able to drive. With your fingertips, you could get someone to come over to your house and drive you to wherever you want to go within Ilorin. The best part is, you can negotiate with the driver and get really pocket friendly prices.
